Is there a better software than Grammarly?

Yes and No. Allow us to explain. Grammarly is a writing assistant that helps you quickly fix grammatical errors, scan your writing for tone of voice and provide suggestions for consistency, and improve clarity in your writing.

What Grammarly doesnt do is create structure, provide in depth feedback for your writing, rewrite, shorten, and expand sentences to improve clarity and make your writing stronger, etc.

From this perspective, we can say there is better software you can use. It all depends on your needs and what your goals are.

For example, some tools that can help you improve your writing would be Wordtune, ProWritingAid, and the Hemingway Editor.

Wordtune is a writing assistant powered by AI that understands the context and semantics of sentences to then provide accurate suggestions. Wortune helps writers fix grammatical errors and misspellings, switch between casual and formal writing, and shorten or expand sentences to make them stronger and easier to understand.

Another interesting feature Wordtune provides is English translations for words in context. These are perfect for foreigners who may not remember a word in English.

For instance, lets say you write Im going paracaidismo this February with aunt Mary. Wordtune will automatically suggest a translation to address the word that doesnt fit the sentence.

We recommend you give Wordtune a try. It should help you quickly and easily fix any grammatical errors in your writing, rephrase sentences to improve clarity, and help you remember words in English if you ever need it.

ProWritingAid is a prominent and popular writing assistant for writers. Its main feature is its report delivery, which provides you insights on your grammar and spelling, sentence variety, readability, how often you use passive voice, emotion tells, unusual dialog, among other things.

This tool is one of the best tools for writers due to its robust feature set. However, thats also a downside as it often feels overwhelming for new writers.

The Hemingway Editor is an online writing assistant that helps make your writing bold and clear. Hemingway highlights long and complex sentences, and sentences that contain grammatical errors. If the highlight is yellow, it means that the sentence is hard to read and if it is red, its too long and complex to understand.

Hemingway also helps identify simple alternatives to sentences and the overuse of adverbs and passive voice. The only disadvantage is that it doesnt actually provide suggestions you can use instead of what you already have.

Thats it. Try all three writing assistants and choose the one that suits you best. If you have more questions about writing and grammar, let us know and well do our best to help.
